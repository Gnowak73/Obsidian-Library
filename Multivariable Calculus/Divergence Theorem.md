A theorem relating a volume integral to a [[Surface Integral]]. It is the 3D version of [[Green's Theorem (flux)]], and it states that:
$$
\int\int\int_{E} \nabla\cdot\vec{F} \ dV = \int\int_{S}\vec{F}\cdot d\vec{S}
$$
