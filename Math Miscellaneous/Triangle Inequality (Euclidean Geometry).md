If $u$ and $v$ are vectors in $R^{n}$, then 
$$
|u+v| \leq |u|+|v|
$$
this relationship can be extended to include [[Function Linear Algebra]] instead of [[Vector Linear Algebra]]. It also shows up in the complex plane.
