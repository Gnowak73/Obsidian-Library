We can change the base of the logarithm. Let us assume that 
$$
\log_{a}x=y
$$
then,
$$
a^{y}=x
$$
If we take the logarithm with base $b$ on both sides
$$
\log_{b}a^{y}=y\log_{b}a=x
$$
If we solve for $y$ we get
$$
y =  \boxed{\log_{a}x =\frac{\log_{b}x}{\log_{b}a}}
$$
